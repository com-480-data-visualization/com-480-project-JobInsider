Job Market Dashboard

Welcome to your interactive guide to the U.S. job market.

Are you entering the workforce? Changing careers? Just curious where the best opportunities lie?

This dashboard is built to help you discover job trends across states.
Explore how demand and salary have evolved through 2023 and 2024, all in one place.

  • Hover over states to dive into salary and job count trends.
  • Use the chart below to explore job activity over time.
  • Slide through time to watch the market shift.

Our goal is to help job seekers, especially new graduates, make informed, confident decisions using real data.

You can return to this screen at any time by pressing the help button in the top right corner.
